About Me

My name is Micheal Hansen, and I'm an aspiring developer from Portland, Oregon, currently training at Epicodus's boot camp.

  • I graduated with a Bachelor's of Science in Mathematics from Portland State University in 2014
  • After college, I worked as a Software Engineer for Beyondsoft Consulting
  • After leaving Beyondsoft, I started learning about programming in depth, ultimately enrolling at Epicodus

My experience at Beyondsoft is what motivated me to seek further training in programming. I was hired for the job based on my knowledge of mathematics, but the job relied a lot on programming, which I had to learn "on the fly". However, I greatly enjoyed the challenge and puzzle of coding, and decided to undergo more comprehensive training in the interest of pursuing it as a career.

Most of my interests resolve around puzzle solving. I love finding a difficult problem, and then working my way towards a solution. This is what lead me to learn how to code, starting originally with python, and now working to learn HTML, CSS, and JavaScript as well. In my free time, I also enjoy puzzles like crosswords and sudoku. I have little time for television any more, but I like to watch NJPW when I can.
